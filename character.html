Character Creation

How to use this section

In this section, you'll build your base character!

Begin by selecting a gender. Next, select "My Choice" or "Random" for each trait. If you choose "Random", a trait will be picked for you and displayed in the Choose Your Own section. Feel free to change these if you'd like! Once you have the set of traits you desire, click SUBMIT TO CHARACTER to lock them in, then CONTINUE TO STATS to move on.

Choose wisely, for these selections may determine the fate of your campaign...
