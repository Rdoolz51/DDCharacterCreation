Instructions

Using the data you preiously submitted, this section will either randomly choose your character's cantrips or you can choose your own!

The initial switch lets you choose if you would like to Randomize your cantrips or you can choose which ones to use.

Depending on your response to the switch, you will either be shown all of the available spells to choose from or you will be shown the randomly chosen spells!

If your class does not have any cantrips to choose from you will not be able to pick any spells!
